Privacy Policy for ESHAM

Last Updated:

Welcome to ESHAM. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our platform.

By accessing or using ESHAM, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from using our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use our platform:

1.1 Personal Information

When you sign up or interact with our services, we collect information such as:

  • Full Name
  • National Saudi ID Number
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Profile picture

1.2 Non-Personal Information

We automatically collect non-personal data, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • IP address
  • Email Address
  • Device information
  • Usage data (e.g., pages visited, time spent on the platform)

1.3 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and improve security. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our services
  • Authenticate user identities
  • Improve platform functionality and security
  • Process transactions and payments
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraudulent activities

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information in the following cases:

3.1 With Authorized Third Parties

  • Payment service providers to process transactions
  • Government or regulatory authorities if required by law
  • Third-party service providers for platform enhancements

3.2 Business Transfers

If ESHAM undergoes a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that process.

3.3 Legal Compliance

We may disclose personal information if required by law, court order, or regulatory authorities.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Data encryption
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security audits

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your login credentials confidential.

5. Your Rights & Choices

As a user of ESHAM, you have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Update Your Information: Modify your account details.
  • Delete Your Data: Request account deletion, subject to legal and compliance restrictions.
  • Opt-Out of Marketing: Unsubscribe from promotional emails.

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@esham.com.

6. Retention of Data

We retain personal data as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. When no longer needed, data is securely deleted.

7. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies before providing personal information.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of ESHAM after changes means you accept the updated policy.
