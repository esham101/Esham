Frequently Asked Questions

What is Esham?

Esham is a platform that simplifies property rentals by connecting landlords and tenants efficiently.

How do I list my property?

Sign up for an account, navigate to the 'List Property' section, and fill in the required details.

How do I search for rental properties?

Use our advanced search filters to find rental properties that meet your requirements.

Is there a fee for using Esham?

Browsing and listing properties on Esham is free. However, premium features may have a small fee.

Can I contact landlords directly?

Yes, once you find a property you're interested in, you can reach out to the landlord via our secure messaging system.

How secure is my personal information?

We take data security seriously and use encryption to protect your personal details.

Esham is an innovative and authorized digital platform that connects landowners with real estate developers through an integrated system designed to simplify and accelerate the development of real estate projects. The platform empowers stakeholders to communicate effectively, submit proposals, and select the best opportunities for mutual benefit.

Disclaimer: Esham is not an investment website and does not offer any financing or direct investment services. Our role is limited to providing a digital platform that connects landowners with real estate developers, facilitating the process of submitting and reviewing development proposals. All transactions are conducted directly between the parties according to the agreed terms, and the platform assumes no responsibility for any risks associated with agreements between the parties.
